## 📌 Introduction

This project focuses on developing essential digital literacy skills required for students in today’s technology-driven world. As a Student Digital Ambassador, the objective is to guide peers in understanding digital tools, maintaining a professional online presence, and practicing safe and responsible internet usage. The project includes multiple tasks such as creating an awareness infographic, building a digital portfolio, exploring coding and collaboration platforms, learning professional email communication, and understanding cybercrime threats and prevention. Through this project, I gained practical knowledge of various digital platforms and improved my ability to communicate and present information effectively in a structured and professional manner.

---

## 🟢 Task 1: Digital Literacy Infographic

For this task, I created a digital literacy awareness infographic using Canva, an online design tool. The infographic explains the concept of digital literacy and its importance for students. It includes key topics such as useful digital tools like Google Docs, GitHub, and LinkedIn, safe internet practices like using strong passwords and avoiding suspicious links, professional online presence, and email etiquette.

The design was created using a clean and simple layout with icons and short bullet points to make the information easy to understand. The aim was to make the infographic visually appealing and informative at the same time.

One challenge I faced was selecting the most relevant information and presenting it in a concise format without overcrowding the design. This task helped me understand how to present information visually and improved my creativity and communication skills.

## 🟢 Task 2: Digital Portfolio

In this task, I created and updated my digital portfolio by setting up profiles on platforms such as GitHub and LinkedIn. GitHub is used for sharing coding projects and collaborating with others, while LinkedIn is a professional networking platform used to connect with peers, recruiters, and industry professionals.

On GitHub, I created a profile README that includes my basic details such as name, branch, and learning goals. On LinkedIn, I added my educational details, including my current degree and institution.

These platforms will play an important role in my academic and professional journey over the next four years. I plan to use GitHub to upload my projects and improve my technical skills, while LinkedIn will help me build a professional network and explore internship opportunities.

This task helped me understand the importance of maintaining a professional digital presence from an early stage.

## 🟢 Task 3: Platforms Exploration

In this task, I explored coding practice platforms and collaboration tools. I created an account on a coding platform and completed a beginner-level challenge to understand how coding problems are structured and solved. This helped me improve my problem-solving skills and gain confidence in coding.

Additionally, I created a Google Form as a Digital Literacy Awareness Quiz containing five questions, including both multiple-choice and short-answer types. This form can be used to test the awareness level of students regarding digital literacy.

I also viewed the responses in Google Sheets, which helped me understand how data can be collected and analyzed efficiently.

These tools are highly useful for academic purposes. Coding platforms help in improving programming skills, while Google Forms and Sheets are useful for conducting surveys, collecting data, and collaborating with others.

## 🟢 Task 4: Email Etiquette

In this task, I learned about professional email communication and social media etiquette. Poor digital communication can lead to misunderstandings and negative impressions. For example, sending an email without a proper subject line or using informal language with a professor can appear unprofessional.

To avoid such situations, it is important to follow proper email structure, including a clear subject line, polite greeting, and professional tone. I also created a checklist of Do’s and Don’ts for responsible social media usage.

This task helped me understand the importance of maintaining professionalism in digital communication and being mindful of how we present ourselves online.

---

## 🟢 Task 5: Cybercrime Awareness

In this task, I explored cybercrime awareness and prevention. I learned about different types of cybercrimes such as phishing and online fraud. What surprised me most was how easily people can become victims of such attacks due to lack of awareness.

Cybercriminals often use fake emails or messages to trick users into sharing personal or financial information. This made me realize the importance of being cautious while using the internet.

As a result of this task, I will adopt safer online habits such as verifying links before clicking, using strong passwords, and enabling two-factor authentication. This task increased my awareness of online threats and how to stay protected.

Each task provided practical knowledge and improved my confidence in using digital platforms. Overall, this project was a valuable learning experience that will help me in my future studies and career.
